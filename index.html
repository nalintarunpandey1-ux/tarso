TARSO – Terrace Automated Rain and Sun Optimizer

Smart weather protection for your outdoor spaces

The Problem We're Solving

Extreme Heat Damage
Intense sunlight fades furniture, wilts plants, and reduces solar panel efficiency by up to 25%.
Unpredictable Rain
Sudden downpours ruin cushions, flood garden beds, and damage electronics without warning.
High Maintenance Costs
Frequent repairs and replacements drain budgets and reduce the lifespan of outdoor setups.

Meet TARSO – Your Intelligent Weather Guardian

TARSO is an affordable, fully automated system that detects and responds to changing weather conditions in real-time. No manual adjustments—just effortless protection for your terrace, 24/7. Designed for urban homeowners, property managers, and small commercial spaces, TARSO combines cutting-edge sensors with smart automation to safeguard what matters most.

How TARSO Works

1. Continuous Monitoring
Advanced sensors track sunlight, rainfall, and ambient temperature every second.
2. Intelligent Processing
The Arduino Uno microcontroller analyzes data and makes instant decisions based on preset thresholds.
3. Automatic Response
The relay and servo motor control the cover and pump automatically based on conditions.
4. Zero Human Effort
The system operates completely autonomously—you never lift a finger.

Technical Components Used

Arduino Uno
The brain of TARSO, processing all sensor inputs and controlling outputs in real-time.
Relay Module
Controls high-power devices like the water pump safely and efficiently.
Servo Motor
Used to open and close the roof automatically during rain or heat.
Water Pump
Activates to irrigate plants when the soil moisture level is low.
Rain Sensor
Detects rainfall and triggers the roof-closing mechanism to protect the terrace.
Soil Moisture Sensor
Monitors soil humidity to control irrigation automatically.
